the helping hand

In this project, the aim is to explore the creative collaboration between human and machine.

The designed boids play a role in order to step away from the artificial nature of the system, functioning as live autonomous beings. With this, I question not only the computer as the creator, with its innate man-made and synthetic aesthetic, but also investigate its redefinition as an organic system.

It critically highlights the innate interpositioning nature of the human being. Rather than simply co-living with other structures, the tendency is to disturb or rearrange its natural order, forcing ourselves into this new environment.
